About Us

From Top to Bottom Cleaning Service is a family owned and operated business that strives to keep your home as clean as theirs! They mainly operate in East Contra Costa County like Antioch and Oakley, but it is not rare to see them as far as Martinez, San Ramon, and Livermore.
The first step in getting a cleaning is setting up a free estimate. This is where you explain to the us what it is you would like done. From there they let you know how much it would roughly cost and when would be the best day to do the cleaning.
