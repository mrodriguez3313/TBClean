About Us

From Top to Bottom Cleaning Service is a family owned and operated business that strives to keep your home as clean as ours! We mainly operate in East Contra Costa County area like Antioch and Oakley, but it is not rare to see us as far as Martinez, San Ramon, and Livermore.

The first step in getting a cleaning is setting up a free estimate. This is where you show us what it is you would like serviced. From there we let you know how much it would cost and when would be the best day to do the cleaning.
